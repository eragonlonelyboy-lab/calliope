# Ritual: Design interview (Phase 2)

Purpose: now, and only now, design vocabulary is allowed. Translate the soul into constraints and appetites. Output: `.calliope/interviews/design.md` + success metrics into STATE.md.

## Rules

- Anchor every question back to a soul-interview answer ("you said the feeling is 'relief': does relief move, or is it still?").
- References beat adjectives. "Clean" means nothing; "like stripe.com but warmer" is a spec.
- Record hates as carefully as loves. A "never do this" is a stronger constraint than a "maybe like this."

## Question set

### Dimensionality & motion
1. Flat 2D, layered 2.5D (parallax/depth), or true 3D? Show what each means with one named example site/app if the client hesitates.
2. Motion appetite, 1-10, where 1 = static print and 10 = the site IS the animation. Then calibrate: "at your number, a page loads: what moves?"
3. Anything that must NEVER move? (Vestibular sensitivity, brand seriousness, data-first contexts.)

### Density & structure
4. Dense and information-rich, or sparse and breathing? Where on that line does their audience-person live?
5. One thing per screen, or an ecosystem per screen?

### References
6. 3 things they love (sites, apps, decks, posters, physical objects, anything). For each: "what exactly do you love about it?" Extract the attribute, not the artifact.
7. 3 things they hate, same extraction. Write these as standing constraints.
8. Competitors' design: who looks best in their market right now, and does that annoy them?

### Medium constraints
9. Hard constraints: existing brand assets that must survive, platform requirements, accessibility mandates, locales/scripts, device floor, file-size or CMS constraints, legal/compliance strings.
10. Who maintains this after handoff, and what can they realistically operate?

### Success metrics (mandatory close)
11. **"How will we know this worked?"** Push to 2-3 measurable criteria with numbers and dates where possible (signups, conversion, demo-requests, "the board stops asking why we look cheap" is acceptable if they name who says it and when). Vague answers get one push: "what would you screenshot to prove it worked?"

## Close

Write `.calliope/interviews/design.md` with sections: dimensionality, motion appetite (number + calibration), density, loved-attributes, hated-constraints, medium constraints, maintenance reality. Write success metrics into `.calliope/STATE.md` under `## Success metrics`. Read the constraint list back; correct until clean. Mark Phase 2 done.
